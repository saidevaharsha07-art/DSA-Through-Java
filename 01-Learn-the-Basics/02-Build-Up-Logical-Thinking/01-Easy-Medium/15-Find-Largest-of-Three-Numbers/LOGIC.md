# 15 Find Largest of Three Numbers

## Logic

Compare three numbers and find the biggest one.

## Steps

1. Take inputs a, b, and c.
2. Check if a is greater than or equal to both b and c.
3. If true, a is largest.
4. Else check if b is greater than or equal to both a and c.
5. If true, b is largest.
6. Otherwise, c is largest.
7. Print the largest number.

## Example

Input: 10, 25, 15

Output: 25 is largest
