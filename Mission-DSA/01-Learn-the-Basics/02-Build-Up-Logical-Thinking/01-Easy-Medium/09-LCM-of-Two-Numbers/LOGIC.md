# 09 LCM of Two Numbers

## Logic

LCM (Least Common Multiple) is the smallest number divisible by both numbers.

## Steps

1. Take inputs a and b.
2. Find the GCD of a and b.
3. Use the formula:
   LCM = (a × b) / GCD
4. Print the LCM.

## Formula

LCM = (a × b) / GCD(a, b)

## Example

Input: 12, 18

Output: 36
